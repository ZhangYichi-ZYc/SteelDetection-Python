This dataset was downloaded from the NEU Metal Surface Defects Database.
It collects six kinds of typical surface defects of hot-rolled steel strips: 
				rolled-in scale (RS)
				patches (Pa)
				crazing (Cr)
				pitted surface (PS)
				inclusion (In)
				scratches (Sc)
The database includes 1,800 grayscale images and 300 samples, each of six typical surface defects.
